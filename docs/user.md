# User Guide: Mastering MemoryDeck

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to use MemoryDeck to accelerate your English learning journey.

---

## üèó Managing Your "Decks"

MemoryDeck organizes vocabulary into **Decks**. 

### Creating a New Deck
1. Navigate to the **"My Decks"** page using the navigation bar.
2. In the **"Create New Deck"** section, enter a descriptive name for your collection (e.g., "Medical Terms," "IELTS 7.0").
3. Click **"Create Deck"**. Your new deck will appear in the list below.

### Adding and Editing Cards
1. Locate your deck in the **"Your Decks"** list.
2. Click on the deck's name to expand it.
3. In the **"Add New Cards"** form:
   - Enter a **Term** (the word you want to learn).
   - Enter a **Definition** (or translation).
4. Click **"Add Row"** to add multiple terms at once.
5. Click **"Save Cards"** to commit them to your deck.
   - *Tip: You can view existing cards in the scrollable list above the entry form.*

## üéì Learning Modes

Memorydeck offers four distinct modes to challenge your retrieval memory.

### 1. Flashcards (SRS)
A classic study tool. Flip the card to reveal the definition. 
- Use the **Easy**, **Good**, or **Hard** buttons to report your difficulty. The app uses this to optimize your review frequency.

### 2. Quiz Mode
Test your recognition. Choose the correct definition for the given word from four multiple-choice options.

### 3. Matching Game
A race against the clock. Click a word and then click its corresponding definition to clear the board.

### 4. Spelling Check (New!)
The ultimate test of pronunciation and spelling. 
- Click the **"Listen"** icon to hear the word spoken aloud.
- Type the word exactly as it sounds.
- This feature uses high-quality **Text-to-Speech** (TTS) engines built into your device.

## üåô Customizing the Theme

MemoryDeck is designed to be easy on the eyes, whether you're studying in a bright coffee shop or late at night.

### Toggling "Night Mode"
1. Locate the **Gear (Settings)** icon in the top-right corner of the header.
2. Click the icon to open the quick settings menu.
3. Click the **"Light Mode/Dark Mode"** toggle.
   - **Light Mode**: High-contrast, clean interface.
   - **Dark Mode**: Sleek "Night Mode" with glassmorphism and reduced blue light.
   - *Your preference is automatically saved and will be applied the next time you open the app.*
